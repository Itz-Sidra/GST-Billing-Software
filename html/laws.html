Understanding GST Law

Goods and Services Tax (GST) is a comprehensive indirect tax levied on the supply of goods and services across India. It replaced multiple indirect taxes and brought uniformity to the tax system.

Key Features of GST

  • Single tax system covering CGST, SGST, and IGST.
  • Elimination of cascading tax effects.
  • Improved compliance through digital records.
  • Input tax credit mechanism.

GST Acts and Rules

The Central Goods and Services Tax (CGST) Act, 2017 governs the levy of GST in India.

The Integrated Goods and Services Tax (IGST) Act regulates inter-state transactions.

Each state has its own SGST Act to manage intra-state transactions.

Ensures states receive compensation for revenue loss due to GST implementation.
