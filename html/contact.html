Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register a complaint about GST services?
You can register a complaint by filling out the contact form on this page, calling our toll-free number 1800-103-4786, or sending an email to complaints@gst.gov.in with details of your issue.
What should I do if I'm facing technical issues with the GST portal?
For technical issues, please select "Technical Issue" in the subject dropdown of the contact form and provide detailed information about the error you're experiencing, including screenshots if possible. Alternatively, you can call our technical helpdesk at +91-120-4888999.
How long does it take to get a response to my query?
We aim to respond to all queries within 48 working hours. Complex issues may take up to 7 working days for resolution. You'll receive an acknowledgment email with a reference number once your query is submitted.
Can I visit the GST office in person for assistance?
Yes, you can visit your nearest GST office during working hours for in-person assistance. It's recommended to book an appointment beforehand through our helpline to minimize waiting time. Please bring relevant documents and your GSTIN (if applicable).
Are there any charges for GST-related assistance or queries?
No, there are no charges for seeking assistance or submitting queries regarding GST. All helpdesk services provided by the government are completely free of cost. Beware of fraudsters asking for payment for GST services.
